About Us

We are reimagining traditional harvesting by creating new ways of picking.

We are entering a new agricultural revolution

Fuelled by innovation the world of argiculture is now ripe for disruption. A combination of key technologies are making what was once impossible a reality.

We are harnessing the power of advances in AI and Robotics

To deliver new solutions to old problems. Recent advances in Deep Neural Networks, computer vision and low cost robotic components are making it feasible to build advanced yet accessible solutions.

We are developing Automated harvesting technologies

That will enable farmers to pick more for a better price, and bring their best fruit to the table

We pick fruit with robots

We are an Australian based robotics company that is enabling farmers to improve the productivity of their farms using Artificial Intelligence and Robotics.

We identify

different types of fruit and their level of ripeness.

We pick

fruits from the tree using custom designed robotic solutions

We deliver

fruit back to the next stage, ready for distribution or storage.
